Our Solution

An AI-Gated Marketplace
Built for Culture NFTs

🤖

AI Gatekeeper

Every asset passes through a FastAPI AI engine. Only high-quality, unique assets receive a cryptographic signature to mint on-chain.

🔐

ECDSA-Signed Minting

Smart contract verifies AI signature on-chain. No signature = no mint. Replay attacks prevented via unique nonces.

💰

70/15/15 Royalty Split

70% Creator · 15% Brand/Organizer · 15% Fankar Treasury — enforced by ERC-2981 on every secondary sale.

📦

IPFS Metadata

All assets and metadata permanently pinned to IPFS via Pinata. Decentralized storage, zero censorship, forever accessible.
